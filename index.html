Safety and Insurance

We are fully insured and maintain the highest level of safety during your commercial painting project. Where required, our employees also use proper harness and safety equipment to eliminate any chance of a workplace incident.

Flexibility – Nights & Weekends. We work around you and your business

Downtime costs money, which is why we work around your schedule. From project start to finish, our crews are courteous, professional and punctual. We make commercial painting a hassle-free experience.

Planning, Executing and Delivery

Our commercial painting process starts with a meeting and initial discovery. We work with you to assess the full needs of your projects. When involved in the beginning, we can often make cost-effective recommendations in reducing time and materials involved in the painting and finishing process. Our process looks like this:

  • Communicate – Listen to your project needs, deadlines and requirements.
  • Plan & Price – We construct a detailed budget and schedule.
  • Execution – The project starts. We work around you and your employees. Flexibility is crucial
  • Follow up & Sign off – We complete a final walkthrough and inspection with you to ensure it meets your standards.

Residential

Read about the Almax interior process from consulation to after sales care. Show/Hide

It all starts with a call and consultation to Almax. Maybe you’ve already nailed down your color palette of choice, or perhaps you need the trained eye of one of our color experts?

We begin with a Almax color expert visiting you for a one-on-one consultation to give you a solid quote and recommendation.

We take you through our process, and offer advice on what colors best accent certain areas or walls of your home. If you already have your colors selected we can simply provide you with an estimate and time frame to complete your project. Either way, we adapt to meet your needs.

Start to finish, you can expect detail and professionalism. Almax professionals always perform and use the following:

  1. Full prep and sanding of imperfections and other interior surfaces to ensure a flawless base in which to apply your new paint.
  2. Move furniture, remove and re-install faceplates / fixtures as required.
  3. Large drop clothes used throughout to protect your flooring and spaces.
  4. High quality low-VOC Paints to reduce odors and smells

Post-Paint, clean-up, inspection and completion

All fixtures, faceplates and furniture are re-installed and placed in their original locations. Once our paint professionals are fully finished we then take you on a walk-through and address any questions or concerns you may have.

We also complete a yearly inspection of our work, at no cost or inconvenience to you. If we spot any problems with the paint or workmanship, we will notify you. It’s all part of the hassle free Almax process.
